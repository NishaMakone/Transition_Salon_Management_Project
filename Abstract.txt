Description:

Transition Salon Management Project is a web based application which helps user to book appointment for salon.
This Saoln management system facilitates effective scheduling of appointments for clients into different-different salons. Also, gives facility to book the employee from whom customer wants to take service and schedule an appointment for customer.

Scope:
Transition Salon Management is a web application. The main objective of this web application is to provide different salon services at one platform.

Modules:
1) Authentication for salon
2) Service Provider Management
3) Appointment Scheduling 
4) Commercial Management
